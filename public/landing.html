Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

BurnRate OS collects only the information necessary to provide our service: your email address, license key, and the financial data you manually enter into the dashboard (expenses, income, subscriptions).

We do not connect to your bank accounts. All financial data is entered manually by you and stored securely in our database.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the BurnRate OS service
  • To send you your license key and activation email
  • To power the AI Financial Advisor with your data
  • To send important service updates

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL). We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

AI Data Usage

When you use the AI Advisor, your financial data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. This data is not used to train AI models. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to these interactions.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@burnrate-os.com.

License

Subscriptions
